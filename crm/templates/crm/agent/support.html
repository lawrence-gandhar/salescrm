Leads Management

This section manages the lead. In this section you can do the following:

  • Assigning leads to agents
  • Tracking the changes made on the leads
  • Deactivating and activating leads
  • Adding Comments and messages for leads
  • Changing lead status

All of the above are explained below in detail.

Edit "STEP 2" Form

This form is "Lead Questionnaire Form". In the Manage section, on clicking the "SHOW LEAD DETAILS" the "Edit Questionnaire" button appears. On clicking it, you will be redirected to the "STEP 2" form for edit. The image below shows the button to redirect.

Add Messages/Comments

After redirection to the message page, on clicking the "Add Message/Comment" a form will appear. Add the message there and submit.

Leads Assignments

After redirection to the lead assignment page, on clicking the pencil icon you will be redirected to the page where you can drag and drop to assign a user to the lead. Here you can also select multiple leads for a status change and even activate or deactivate multiple leads by clicking the "More Actions Button". To assign a user just drag and drop in the assigned area and click on the "SAVE" button. To remove a user from the assigned list, click on the trash button in the user list, it will turn to red, then click "SAVE" button to save the list.
